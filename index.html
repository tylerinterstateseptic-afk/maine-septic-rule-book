Help & Setup Guide

Everything you need to get started and share the app

Step 1 — Get a Free API Key

  • 1On your phone or computer, open a browser and go to console.anthropic.com
  • 2Create a free account or sign in
  • 3Tap API Keys in the left menu, then Create Key
  • 4Give it any name (e.g. "Septic Rules"), then copy the key — it starts with sk-ant-
  • 5You will need to add a small amount of credit ($5 minimum) under Billing. Each question costs fractions of a cent.
  • 6Come back here, tap Setup, paste your key, and tap Load Rule Book. It takes about 2 minutes to index all 140+ pages — only once.
Your key is saved permanently on this device. Tap Setup → Show to view or copy it later as a backup.

Step 2 — Add to Your Phone Home Screen

  • ●Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ at the top right → tap Add to Home screen → tap Add
  • ●iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom → scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen → tap Add
Once added, it opens like an app. Your setup and API key stay saved — no need to redo anything.

Step 3 — Share With Someone

Send them this link or have them scan the QR code below:

https://tylerinterstateseptic-afk.github.io/maine-septic-rule-book/

Scan to open on any phone

They will need their own API key (Step 1). The rule book loads automatically after setup.
